The Shift from Ranking to Citation

In the legacy SEO era, we fought for "Position 1." In the AEO era, we fight for the "Citation Source." When a user asks an LLM a question about your industry, the AI doesn't show a list of links; it synthesizes an answer.

If your data isn't structured for LLM consumption, you don't exist.

### The Trinity of AEO

1. **Semantic Density:** Using highly specific, technical language that helps the model categorize your expertise.
2. **Schema Injection:** Using advanced JSON-LD to "pre-digest" your content for the crawler.
3. **The Logic Loop:** Creating "Source-Heavy" content that provides factual, verifiable data points that AI models crave for grounding.

> "AI models are lazy. They will always cite the source that is easiest to parse and most logically sound." — Marketworth Intelligence
