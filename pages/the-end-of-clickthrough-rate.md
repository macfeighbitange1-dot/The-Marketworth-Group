## The Zero-Click Reality
In 2026, 85% of queries are resolved within the AI Interface (Perplexity/SearchGPT). If a user doesn't click your link but the AI says "According to Marketworth Group...", you have won.

## Measuring Citation Share
We track how often an LLM mentions your brand when prompted with "top-of-funnel" industry questions. 
* **Legacy Metric:** Ranking #1 on Google.
* **Sovereign Metric:** Being the primary source for the "AI Summary."

## Evolution
We don't optimize for humans who browse; we optimize for the models that summarize.
