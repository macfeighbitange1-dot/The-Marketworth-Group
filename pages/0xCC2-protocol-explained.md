## What is 0xCC2?
The 0xCC2 Protocol is a multi-layered optimization framework designed to maximize **Model Trust Scores**. 

## The Three Pillars
1.  **Semantic Saturation:** Ensuring 90% of your content consists of high-value technical entities.
2.  **Structural Integrity:** Validating that every page has a corresponding entry in the `llms.txt` and `sitemap.xml`.
3.  **Grounding Loops:** Providing verifiable data points that match the AI's internal training weights.

## Deployment
Implementing 0xCC2 requires a total decoupling from legacy SEO "keyword stuffing" and a pivot to **Technical Entity Architecture**.
